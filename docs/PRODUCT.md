# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Curious people interested in deep, sometimes polarizing conversations about science, technology, and philosophy. They are looking for intellectual stimulation, lively debate, and thought experiments.

## Product Purpose
To serve as an interactive debate generator ("Do'h - Spark an argument") that provides thought-provoking questions and ethical dilemmas. It exists to break the ice and trigger deep discussions. Success looks like users finding the UI engaging, fast, and the content genuinely discussion-worthy.

## Brand Personality
Bold, confrontational yet playful, raw. Neo-brutalist. It leans into high contrast, sharp edges, and a lack of generic polish. It embraces a bit of chaos (like the "CHOOSE VIOLENCE" mode).

## Anti-references
Absolutely no glassmorphism. No soft, airy, generic "AI SaaS" aesthetics. No smooth gradients or subtle blurred drop shadows. No corporate blandness or typical AI tooling tells.

## Design Principles
1. **Embrace the brutal:** Let borders be thick, typography be loud, and shadows be hard.
2. **Action over elegance:** Interactions should feel responsive, punchy, and deliberate (like the zippy glitch/scramble), not floaty or overly smooth.
3. **Contrast is king:** Ensure typography and interactive elements stand out aggressively.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Ensure standard web accessibility best practices (adequate contrast for brutalist colors, keyboard navigability). Incorporate `prefers-reduced-motion` considerations for the glitch and text scramble effects so users with vestibular sensitivities are not overwhelmed.
